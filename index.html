Welcome To My Website

I began designing this website as a project for one of my classes in the business school at The George Washington University. Management and systems info gave us the basis we needed to learn about developing websites using html/css. This website is the first attempt that I have made to put these skills to the test and any feedback would be very appreciated.

The website you are currently viewing was designed using atom and github desktop, two platforms I was completely unfamiliar with prior to the class. The website was first launched in the beginning of December, during my fall semester of junior year. Since the launch of this website, I have been working on learning more about styles and formats,as well as javascript. The website will give you a better idea about who I am and what I have been doing during my time at The George Washington University. If you have any questions there is contact information located on the links to the right. I will try to get back to you in an efficient and timely manner. Have fun exploring the website!
