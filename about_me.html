About Me

You may ask yourself why the question of the New York City skyline above if I currently live in Washington D.C. The answer goes back to my childhood and where I grew up. I was born in Jersey city, New Jersey, which neighbors manhattan. New York City holds a place in my heart like no other, my first job was at a retail store in New York City when I was in highscool.

During my years living in New Jersey, I attended Saint Peters Preparatory, a jesuit high school located in downtown Jersey City. Attending school in the downtown spurred my interests in the finance industry due to the numerous investment banks located in the area. Saint Peter's Prep provided me with numerous skills that allowed me to develop into a well rounded man.

My time at Prep was spent playing football and rugby, while holding a position on the stock market club. Ater graduating high school, I left New Jersey to attend The George Washington University. I am currently a junior studying finance and economics, while being an active member of Sigma Phi Epsilon. For more information about me visit my Linkedin profile.
